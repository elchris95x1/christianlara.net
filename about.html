My Background

My name is Christian Lara. I make computer applications. I've been coding since 2014. I was born in Mexico, but I moved to Waco Tx in 2013.

My interest in computers grew during my first summer in the US. I did not have any friends, but I had a laptop that I had bought with the savings I had earned while working in Mexico. At first I did not use the laptop, but when I discovered a public wifi near my house... Well magic hapenned.

What I Do

I design and code applications from scratch that can solve a problem for a person or company, from coding apps and websites, to setting up databases and creating images or videos for your blog posts.

Besides designing and coding apps, my main priority is to offer solutions to your business problems. A nice shiny new program sounds great, but I'm all about creating the right solution to help push your business to the next level.
